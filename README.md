An all-in-one interactive web platform designed to centralize, manage, and promote college events, connecting students, college authorities, and event coordinators for seamless participation and administration.

### Short Description / Purpose
EventVerse serves as a centralized hub for college students and teachers to gather and discover all college events in one location. Its primary goal is to democratize access to technical education and opportunities by providing a platform where colleges can promote their events widely, and students can easily discover and participate in learning opportunities beyond their campus.

### Features / Highlights
**Business Problem**
Students often miss out on valuable college events due to scattered information across different college websites and platforms. Event organizers lack a single, large platform to promote their events to a wider, interested audience.

**Goal of the Project**
To create an efficient, transparent, and centralized event management and discovery platform that increases student participation in technical and learning events while simplifying the administrative process for college coordinators.
